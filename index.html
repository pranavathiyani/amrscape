Data Sources

🗂
CARD 2025
Comprehensive Antibiotic Resistance Database — ARO accessions, gene names, resistance mechanisms, curated ESKAPE resistomes
🧬
UniProt REST API
Protein sequences, Pfam domain cross-references and protein names for each AMR gene (live API)
🔗
STRING (live API)
Protein–protein interaction scores ≥0.70 used as co-resistance network edges per ESKAPE taxon
💊
WHO AWARE 2025
Access / Watch / Reserve antibiotic classification. 2025 edition — updated every 2 years by WHO Expert Committee
⚠️
WHO Priority Pathogens 2024
Bacterial pathogens of public health importance — Critical / High priority classification for ESKAPE organisms
★
Therapeutic Escape Index (TEI) — Novel metric
% of WHO-classified drug classes where a co-infection scenario has no shared susceptibility. Computed from CARD + WHO AWARE 2025. Developed for AMRscape.
